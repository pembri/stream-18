Privacy Policy

At STREAM 18, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our website.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information unless you voluntarily provide it, such as when using our contact forms or admin tools. We may collect non-personal data like browser type and access times to improve our service.

2. Cookies

We use essential cookies to enhance site navigation and save your preferences (e.g., login tokens for the admin panel). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

3. Third-Party Content

Our site includes embedded videos from third-party platforms (YouTube, Vimeo, etc.). These providers may collect data about your interaction with the embedded content according to their own privacy policies.

4. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
